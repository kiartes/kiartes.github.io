Slime Clicker

Your goal is to catch all kinds of slugs and upgrade their levels to get more gold from them! - There are several categories of rarity in the game. - Slimes of different rarity categories appear with different chances. - Each slug has its own timer, during which you need to catch the slug. - The faster you catch a slug, the more gold coins you get from it. - The level of the slug determines how much health it will have and the number of coins produced. Don't forget to upgrade your hunter to catch slugs faster and also level them up to get even more gold coins!
